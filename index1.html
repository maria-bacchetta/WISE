About WISE

Women in Science and Engineering (WISE) is a student organization at Indiana Tech dedicated to supporting and empowering women pursuing careers in STEM. Through mentorship, collaborative projects, workshops, and networking opportunities, WISE creates a welcoming community where students can explore innovation, leadership, and professional growth in science and technology.
